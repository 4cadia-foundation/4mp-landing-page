Connecting Providers and Consumers of tokenized assets and services

What is 4MP?

A decentralized platform that aims to provide the meeting between assets providers and tokenized services to consumers, in order to provide an inclusive and fair market for anyone, through a search mechanism coupled with a reputation system to enhance security in both sides.

How it works

The consumer/provider will be able to rely on a variety of mechanisms that provide security and clarity in deciding which path to take: providers can create different pages for different products and markets, such pages and products will be available to consumers based on categories and reputation, the reputation mechanism will be decentralized, that means fully transparent and supported by feedback from stakeholders.

Users will be able to rely on a transaction control system supported by blockchain-recorded data through SmartContracts and a decentralized identity system, technologies that aggregate security as well as privacy on both parts.

What we believe?

DECENTRALIZATION

With blockchain technology, where everything is seen and managed by all the users.

REPUTATION

4MP Plataform has the shared reputation system, where the buyer rates who provided the service and all users can see it.

P2P

There is no middle-man, so the buyer deals directly with the seller and only both profit from it

Roadmap

Development of the code is happening using agile methodology and it is coherent with the ideals of decentralization and open-source movements. The development roadmap is aggressive and focused on connecting buyers and sellers of lower income in a responsible fashion.
